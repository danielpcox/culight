Light 11 is a night of praise, exhortation, prayer for the nations, and resources for Summer 2011 mission trips. Our hope is that Christians in Ithaca and all over the world would know God's heart for the nations and be encouraged to pray, send and go.
"Being a Nobody for Christ"

This year, we have invited Dr. Michael Oh to come speak on being a nobody for Christ. Michael was born and raised in the Philadelphia area, and he attended the University of Pennsylvania as an undergraduate where he also received his Master's degree in Education and his PhD in Leadership and Cultural Anthropology. Michael received a Master of Divinity degree at Trinity Evangelical Divinity School and a Master's degree in East Asian Studies at Harvard University with a concentration in Japanese anthropology and sociology. In 2005, Michael founded Christ Bible Seminary, the fastest growing seminary in Japan. Michael is a PCA Teaching Elder in the Philadelphia Presbytery and the youngest member of the executive leadership of the Lausanne Committee for World Evangelization. He has spoken at Urbana and Desiring God conferences.

What is the history behind Light 11?
Briefly put, Light 11 is the fusing of two missions mobilization/information/prayer nights that were held in the past at Cornell University, which included KCCE's CUStraightAhead and the Student Volunteer Movement (SVM2). CU Light was born in 2009.
When/where is Light 11?
Light 11 will be held on March 12, 2011 in BioTech G10 from 7:00 - 9:30PM. (Here is a Google Map to the BioTech building, which is between and behind the Lynah skating rink and Teagle hall.)

What is "Prayer for the Nations"?
From roughly 8:45 to 9:30, we will have 12 "country stations" set up around the room with information and prayer requests for each nation, as well as pictures and videos. During the prayer time, attendees will be able to move freely around the room to intercede for the nations.
Welcome to the website of Light 11!
Based on the missions event of the same name, this website exists to continue the vision of Light 11 throughout the rest of the year by educating, encouraging, supporting, and providing resources about world missions to the body of Christ in Ithaca and beyond.
On this site you will find:
  • Information and prayer requests for nations
  • Opportunities to go on missions (summer 2011 and longterm)
  • Testimonies from Ithacans who have gone on trips in the past
  • Support information for those who will be going on missions
  • Other resources such as pictures, videos, and noteworthy quotes pertaning to missions
  • Links to various groups affiliated with Light 11
  • Registration for our email list
  • In the future, we hope to set up an online fund to support those going on mission trips from Cornell and Ithaca.
