In him was life, and the life was the light of men. The light shines in the darkness and the darkness has not overcome it. John 1:4-5
Light 09 is a night of praise, exhortation, prayer for the nations, and resources for Summer 2009 mission trips. Our hope is that Christians in Ithaca and all over the world would know God's heart for the nations and be encouraged to pray, send and go.
What is the history behind Light 09?
Briefly put, Light 09 is the fusing of two missions mobilization/information/prayer nights that were held in the past at Cornell University, which included KCCE's CUStraightAhead and the Student Volunteer Movement (SVM2).
When/where is Light 09?
Light 09 will be held on February 21, 2009 in BioTech G10 from 7:00 - 9:30PM. (Here is a Google Map to the BioTech building, which is between and behind the Lynah skating rink and Teagle hall.)
 
What's the schedule for the night?
7:00-7:20   Worship
7:20-8:00   Exhortation (Elyse Lee '08, Pioneers International staff)
8:00-8:10   Response/prayer and Video
8:10-9:00   Prayer for the Nations
9:00-9:30   Closing Worship and Prayer
What is "Prayer for the Nations"?
From roughly 8:00 to 9:00, we will have 12 "country stations" set up around the room with information and prayer requests for each nation, as well as pictures and videos. During the prayer time, attendees will be able to move freely around the room to intercede for the nations.
Welcome to the website of Light 09!
Based on the missions event of the same name, this website exists to continue the vision of Light 09 throughout the rest of the year by educating, encouraging, supporting, and providing resources about world missions to the body of Christ in Ithaca and beyond.
On this site you will find:
  • Information and prayer requests for nations
  • Opportunities to go on missions (summer 2009 and longterm)
  • Testimonies from Ithacans who have gone on trips in the past
  • Support information for those who will be going on missions
  • Other resources such as pictures, videos, and noteworthy quotes pertaning to missions
  • Links to various groups affiliated with Light 09
  • Registration for our email list
  • In the future, we hope to set up an online fund to support those going on mission trips from Cornell and Ithaca.
