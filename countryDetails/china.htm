Answers to Prayer for China
  • Praise the Lord for the growth of the Church in China since 1977, which has no parallels in history. The 1,266,000 Protestant members in 1949 had become 17m members in 2000 as well as a much larger uncounted, but estimated, 45m house church Christians.

The persecution purified and indigenized the Church and has inured it to successive waves of further repression and government efforts to weaken or destroy it.

The People's Republic of China
“The most populous country in the world”

For centuries, China was the world’s leader in arts and sciences. However, after World War II, the Communists under Mao Zedong established an autocratic socialist system that, while ensuring China's sovereignty, imposed strict controls over everyday life and cost the lives of tens of millions of people. After 1978, his successor Deng Xiaoping and other leaders focused on market-oriented economic development and by 2000 output had quadrupled. For much of the population, living standards have improved dramatically and the room for personal choice has expanded, yet political controls remain tight.
