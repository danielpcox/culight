Haiti
“Poorest state in the West”

Haiti is the western third of the island of Hispaniola shared with the Dominican Republic. Since 1804, after becoming the first black republic in the world after a slave revolt, Haiti has experienced a history of bloodshed and dictatorships. Elections have been corrupt even after the US intervened in 1994 in an attempt to establish a fairer government. In 2010, Haiti experienced devastating earthquakes which killed many people. Reports state that the death toll could exceed 200,000. Many are without clean water, food, and shelter.
