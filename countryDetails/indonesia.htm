Indonesia
“The nation with the largest Muslim population worldwide”

Indonesia is a string of 17,000 islands stretching between Thailand and the Northern tip of Australia. Indonesia has about 170 million Muslims and 30 million Christians. Although Christianity is one of the five recognized choices of religion that citizens must choose, Christians are highly discriminated against. Many are in jail and under false charges.

In 1997, Indonesia was badly affected by the economic crisis of Asia. As a consequence, about half the population now lives under sever poverty. Aside from the economic crisis, Indonesia has faced a series of natural disasters such as the 2004 tsunami which resulted in 160,000 deaths.
