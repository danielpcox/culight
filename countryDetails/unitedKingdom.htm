United Kingdom
"The world’s first industrialized nation”

The United Kingdom is a union of countries: England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland. The British Empire once covered a quarter of the globe and the British Navy “ruled the waves” Throughout the twentieth century the empire dissolved and Britain has made the uncomfortable adjustment from a world power to a reluctant European nation. Conflict in Northern Ireland, between the Irish and the British, has been brewing for hundreds of years. Thirty years of guerrilla violence have made the process of finding peace difficult.
