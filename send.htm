Ways to Give
Below is a sampling of the missions organizations through which students from Cornell and Ithaca have previously gone on missions trips. Please contact the person listed with each organization if you have any questions or concerns about the organization, previous summer trips and experiences, etc.!
Gospel for Asia
Founded by K.P. Yohanan in 1979, Gospel for Asia’s mission is “to be devout followers of Christ and to fulfill the Great Commission among the unreached in Asia through training, sending out and assisting qualified laborers in partnership with the Body of Christ.” GFA trains and sends native missionaries because they have proven extremely effective.
www.gfa.org
Specific Area: Asia, Unreached People Groups
Support: Training and sending native missionaries
Your Local Church
Support short-term and long-term teams and individuals by giving financially and praying for those going on missions. Check the Light website for those currently going on missions from Cornell/Ithaca.
