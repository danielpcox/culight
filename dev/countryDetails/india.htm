India
"The spiritual home of Hinduism"

More than a billion people live in this country which dominates South Asia. Recently they have suffered unusual weather patterns such as the 2004 tsunami and the 2005 quake. Many people still have no homes to rebuild their lives in.  As the spiritual home of Hinduism, 96% of the 820 million people in the world who follow Hinduism live in India. The whole of Indian society is based on Hinduism, which divides everyone into different "castes" (classes).

India's economy has grown quickly in the last ten years but is also home to many of the world's poorest people. Over 600 million people in India live in deep poverty and another 300 million don't have enough to live on each day. Yet in the past ten years, poverty has been reduced by 10%.
