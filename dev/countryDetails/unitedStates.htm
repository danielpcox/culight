 Keeping The United States in Prayer
  • Nominalism has been growing among U.S. Christians. Pray for renewed passion and a heart of repentance so that Christians can have revival in their spiritual walks with God.
  • The U.S. remains a strong superpower in the world. Pray for guidance and wisdom for its leaders especially in this time of economic crisis and war.

The United States of America
"The land of the free and home of the brave”

The inscription on the Statue of Liberty reads “Give me your poor, your huddled masses yearning to breathe free”. The dream of freedom and opportunity has drawn people from all over the world to settle in America; it now has the greatest variety of ethnic groups of any nation in history.

The USA has grown to be one of the most powerful economies in the world and is home to some of the world’s most successful entrepreneurs, but 35 million American citizens live below the poverty line.
